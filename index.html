Pack 399/499 family camp: Instructions and Sign-up link

  1. The cost of the camp is $35 for every participant.
  2. The camp check-in is at Friday March 25 at 05:00PM
  3. The Camp check-out is at Sunday March 27 by 11:59AM
  4. Camp volunteers will provide 3 meals + 1 snack for Saturday, and Sunday breakfast.
  5. Camp will include campfire programs for Friday and Saturday nights
  6. Camp is open to scouts and their families from Pack 399/499.
  7. Cub Scouts will need to be accompanied by a direct member of their families (adult sibling, mother, father).
  8. All youth participants in BB-gun/Archery activities will need to sign the shooting sports waiver form and hand it in during the time for payments. Form can be found here.
  9. If this is your first year, check the list of required gear.
  10. Safety guidelines can be found here.
  11. Tent Setup and Wildlife Management instructions here.
  12. Payments are to be done directly to your unit's treasurer during den meetings or other published times.
Required items to purchase
  1. Mandatory Scout’s official shirt with US, Scout, SVMBA emblem/patches
  2. Mandatory Scout’s handbook,
  3. Den Leader Guide (if Den Leader or assistant),
  4. Optional -- Scout pants, hat, neckerchief, etc
  5. Optional - Parent scout shirt/pants (See store details below. Items 1 and 2 above should be within $75. Scout store is currently running 10% discount.)
They can be purchased from Scout Store or Merry Mart. Merry Mart might be cheaper but may not have all the items in stock.
